Mission

The mission of Pacific Sound Brass is to educate and entertain through the performance of quality brass literature.

Through inventive concerts, engaging programs, and original musical arrangements, the ensemble brings a fresh perspective to instrumental music.

Based in San Diego, Pacific Sound Brass has served audiences through church series, educational outreach, retirement community programs, and special event performances.
