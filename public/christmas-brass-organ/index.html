Details for the 11th annual holiday concert presentation.

Holiday concert

11th Annual Christmas Brass & Organ Concert

Monday, December 1, 2025
7:00 pm

First Presbyterian Church Downtown
320 Date Street
San Diego, CA 92101

Featuring the San Diego Christmas Brass Ensemble, The Swing Kittens vocal trio, Alex Miniak as narrator, and special guest Bobby Cressey, organist for the San Diego Padres, Gulls, and Sockers.

A beloved holiday tradition continues with seasonal favorites, joyful jazz arrangements, audience singalong, and a few musical surprises — all performed by a massive collection of Southern California’s finest musicians.

Produced by RB Anthony and conducted by Ryan Beard, this is a family-friendly concert designed to brighten the season with brass, organ, and a warm communal atmosphere.

Watch on the First Presbyterian Church YouTube channel

Pacific Sound Brass presents polished brass chamber music for concert halls, churches, community programs, and special events throughout San Diego and beyond.
